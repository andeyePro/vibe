- [x] **task_008 — vibe: drain clipboard scratch on exit (race fix) (2026-04-25)** — landed in 1 `/vs` cycle. User-reported bug: `/expaste` then `/exit` left the Mac clipboard unchanged. Diagnosis: the EXIT trap in `/workspace/vibe` killed the host-side `vibe-copy-watcher.sh` before the watcher's 0.5s poll loop saw the just-written `/workspace/.vibe/copy-latest.txt` (or before the in-flight `cp + pbcopy` chain completed). New variables `CLIP="$WORKSPACE/.vibe/copy-latest.txt"` and `WATCHER_SEED_MTIME=$(stat -f %m "$CLIP" 2>/dev/null || stat -c %Y "$CLIP" 2>/dev/null || echo 0)`, both block-scoped inside the existing `if Darwin && pbcopy` block, captured AFTER `mkdir -p` and BEFORE the watcher backgrounds.
