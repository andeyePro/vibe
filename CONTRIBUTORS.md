# Contributors

Who builds vibe, how you get on this list, and what the list is for.

## Why this file matters

vibe is an [andeye](https://andeye.com) product, and andeye's standing promise is that if vibe ever grows features that cost money to run (and therefore a paid tier), major contributors earn a share of that revenue, scaled by the value they added. This file is the ledger that promise operates on: it records who contributed and roughly what, in public, as it happens – not reconstructed after money shows up.

Honest today-state: vibe has no revenue and nothing is accruing. The promise is about the future, and the assessment mechanism (how "value added" is weighed) will be defined openly, in this repo, before any money flows. What will never change: no per-customer extraction, no enshittification. See the [contributor guide](CONTRIBUTING.md) for how to contribute.
